Time-Saving Work Wardrobe


Finding inspiration for work outfits is never easy. You want something comfortable enough for the commute but smart enough for that last-minute meeting. To help you organise your outfits, team White Stuff share their work wardrobe secrets and how to plan your workwear looks ahead of schedule.

THE
STATEMENT
MAKER

Jo Marsden,

" I'm all about the statement jacket this season, it's the one item of clothing that can effortlessly pull your look together. Throw over jeans and tee to turn a simple outfit into something stand-out, in an instant."

GROWN-UP
MINIMALIST

Cathy Hogan,
Buyer

"Everyone needs some simple,pared-back pieces in their wardrobe that they can mix and match with for those easy dressing days. I'd pair these with some classic denim pieces for a more relaxed off duty look and layer them up with some cute knits for a por of colour."

THE
DRESS FIEND

Emma Colthorpe,
Head of Marketing

"For me it's all about finding one or two key pieces and building your work wardrobe around them. Why a dress? Well, it's the easiest thing to throw on at 7am."
